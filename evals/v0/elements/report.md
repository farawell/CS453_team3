## elements ([json](./evals\v0\elements/report.json))


### [prompty](./elements.prompty)

`````md

---
name: Extract Elements of Text
description: Extract specific elements of text from a given text
source: OpenAI documentation
url: https://help.openai.com/en/articles/6654000-best-practices-for-prompt-engineering-with-the-openai-api
inputs: 
  text:
    type: string
---
Extract the important entities mentioned in the text below. First extract all company names, then extract all people names, then extract specific topics which fit the content and finally extract general overarching themes

Desired format:
Company names: <comma_separated_list_of_company_names>
People names: -||-
Specific topics: -||-
General themes: -||-

Text: {{text}}

`````


### [t](./intent.txt)

`````txt
Extract specific elements of text from a given text.
`````


### [c.txt](./input_spec.txt)

`````txt
The input must be a string.  
The string can include multiple sentences or paragraphs.  
The string should include references to company names.  
The string should include references to people names.  
The string should discuss specific topics within its content.  
The string should have overarching themes that can be identified.
`````


### [tests.txt](./baseline_tests.txt)

`````txt
Company names: Apple, Google
People names: Tim Cook, Sundar Pichai
Specific topics: technology innovation, corporate strategy
General themes: advancements in technology, leadership in tech industry

Text: Apple and Google have been at the forefront of technology innovation. Tim Cook, the CEO of Apple, and Sundar Pichai, the CEO of Google, have been pivotal in shaping the corporate strategies of their respective companies.
`````


### [](./rules.txt)

`````txt
1: The output must begin with the phrase "Company names:" followed by a space and then a comma-separated list of company names extracted from the given text.  
2: The output must include the phrase "People names:" followed by a space and then a comma-separated list of people names extracted from the given text.  
3: The output must include the phrase "Specific topics:" followed by a space and then a comma-separated list of specific topics that fit the content of the given text.  
4: The output must include the phrase "General themes:" followed by a space and then a comma-separated list of general overarching themes from the given text.  
5: The entities in the output should be clearly categorized into company names, people names, specific topics, and general themes, each on a separate line after the respective labels.  
6: If a category (like company names, people names, specific topics, or general themes) has no entries extracted from the text, it should still be present in the output with an empty list following the colon.
`````
